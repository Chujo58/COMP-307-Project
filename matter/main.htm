SOCS Booking Tool

Welcome to Bit Benders Booking Tool

The Bit Benders Booking Tool is a custom-built platform designed for McGill's professors, TAs, and students to make scheduling appointments faster, simpler, and more efficient. This website was created as part of the SOCS Website Competition and offers a modern, flexible solution for booking appointments, managing courses, and handling requests. Our platform provides two unique user experiences depending on your role at McGill.

How It Works

The Bit Benders Booking Tool offers a seamless process for booking, managing, and tracking appointments. Here's a video for how it works: Link to Youtube.

Our mission is to make appointment scheduling fast, clear, and stress-free for the McGill community. Join us and experience the simplicity and efficiency of Bit Benders Booking Tool.

For Students

Students can explore a list of available courses with active staff availability. With built-in filtering options, students can quickly locate the course and staff they need. Once they choose a course, they can view a list of staff members for that course and book appointments based on the staff's available time slots. If no available slots match their needs, students can send a custom request to the staff, which will appear in the staff's Pending Requests section.

  • Browse Available Courses – Use the filters to search for specific courses by Course Tag and Course ID.
  • Select a Course and Staff – View the list of staff associated with the course and select a staff member.
  • View Staff Availability – View the availability calendar and book an appointment in an open slot. If an appointment outside of the staff's availability, it will send a request to the staff.

    • For Staff

    Staff members have access to a personalized dashboard with tools to manage courses, availability, and pending booking requests. They can:

  • Manage Courses – Add or remove yourself from courses and see the list of current courses.
  • View Your Weekly Calendar – View upcoming appointments and availability.
  • Create Availability – Add open time slots where students can book appointments.
  • Review Requests – View, accept, or deny requests from students who want to meet outside your availability.

    • Our goal is to provide McGill staff and students with an intuitive and effective booking experience, ensuring that time is spent on learning, not logistics. Whether you’re scheduling a one-time meeting or managing recurring office hours, Bit Benders Booking Tool is here to make it happen.
